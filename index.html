Who Are We

We’re Non-Extinct, a group of 4 friends who decided to start a software firm together. We thrive in the unconventional and are firm in the belief that innovation takes risk, courage, sleepless nights and a lot of coffee. We aim to be the continental leader in tech innovation and embracing diversity at social, economic and intellectual levels. In January 2021, we officially became Non-Extinct Software Laboratories (Cue the applause).

What We Do

We create websites and software tailored to the needs of your business by:

  • Creating products that cater to your users’ needs by ensuring that the right problem is identified in order to design a digital solution that is effective through the human-centred design approach.

  • Using Agile to manage the project so that we build the important parts first to ensure that we complete the product quickly and successfully.
